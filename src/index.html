RLin.eu - Robin van der Linde

Hi! I'm Robin, a 20 year old student and developer, passionate about OpenStreetMap and maps in general. When I'm done studying, I'll usually start mapping on OpenStreetMap, or if I'm feeling overly ambitious I might even do some more programming. Apart from that I also occasionally play a variety of games. Other hobbies include CAD and 3d-printing, electronics, making things in general and cycling.

If you want to learn more about me, and what I do, you've come to the right place. On this website you can find some basic information about me and some of my current and past projects. If still want to know more, feel free to contact me.
